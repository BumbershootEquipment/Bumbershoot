About Us

Adventure Equipment delivered to city dwellers
Our Story: Bumbershoot is the result of one conversation between the founders. Growing up with camping as primary weekend activity, we wondered why there wasn't an inexpensive and convenient service to rent high quality equipment for a weekend to escape the city. Before long, Bumbershoot was founded, making the weekend escape easier than ever.
