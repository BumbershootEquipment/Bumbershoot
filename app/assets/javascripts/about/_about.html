About Us

Our Story:

Bumbershoot is the result of a single conversation between the founders. Growing up with camping as primary weekend activity, we wondered why there wasn't an inexpensive and convenient service to rent high quality equipment for a weekend to escape the city.

Before long, Bumbershoot was founded, making the weekend escape easier than ever.
